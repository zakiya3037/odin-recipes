Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy cheese, and herbs.

It is baked until golden brown and is perfect for family meals or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil the lasagna noodles.
  2. Cook the ground beef.
  3. Mix the beef with tomato sauce.
  4. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheese.
  5. Bake for about 45 minutes.
  6. Allow it to cool before serving.
