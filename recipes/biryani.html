Biryani

Description

Experience the rich taste of authentic Chicken Biryani, where every grain of fragrant basmati rice is infused with aromatic spices and layered with tender, juicy chicken. Slow-cooked to perfection, this classic dish delivers a delightful balance of flavor, aroma, and texture in every bite.

Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or simply craving something extraordinary, our Chicken Biryani is the perfect choice. Served hot and bursting with traditional flavors, it's a meal that satisfies your appetite and leaves you wanting more.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken, cut into medium-sized pieces
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 3 tablespoons biryani masala
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil or ghee
  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped
  • Fresh mint leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 cups water

Steps

  1. Prepare the Rice: Wash the basmati rice thoroughly and soak it in water for 30 minutes.
  2. Cook the Onions: Heat oil or ghee in a large pot and fry the sliced onions until they turn golden brown.
  3. Add Aromatics: Stir in the ginger-garlic paste and cook for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Cook the Chicken: Add the chicken, tomatoes, yogurt, turmeric powder, red chili powder, biryani masala, and salt. Cook until the chicken is tender and coated in the spices.
  5. Boil the Rice: In another pot, boil the soaked rice until it is about 70% cooked. Drain the water.
  6. Layer the Biryani: Spread the partially cooked rice over the chicken mixture. Sprinkle chopped coriander and mint leaves evenly over the rice.
  7. Steam the Biryani: Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid and cook on low heat for 20–25 minutes so the flavors blend together.
  8. Serve: Gently mix the biryani, garnish with fried onions if desired, and serve hot with raita or a fresh salad.
