pizza

Description

Pizza is a classic Italian dish made with a baked dough base topped with tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and a variety of delicious toppings. It is loved around the world for its crispy crust, cheesy texture, and rich flavors.

The image portrays a freshly baked pizza with a golden-brown crust, melted cheese, and colorful toppings. It highlights the appetizing appearance of the dish, making it an excellent choice for a homemade meal or a gathering with family and friends.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1/2 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup sliced onions
  • 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup black olives
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • 1 teaspoon chili flakes (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Place the pizza dough on a baking tray or pizza pan.
  3. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle a generous layer of shredded mozzarella cheese.
  5. Add the bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, and black olives.
  6. Season with oregano, chili flakes, and drizzle with olive oil.
  7. Bake for 12–15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  8. Remove from the oven, slice the pizza, and serve hot.
